Welcome to Snapchat 2.O!

Tap the 📸 button to take a snap, 🎥 for a video, or 🔄 for a boomerang.

Double-tap the video feed to cycle filters, or scroll the filter carousel at the bottom to choose a filter.

Edit snaps with drawing, stickers, or text (movable!). Use 🗑️ to erase stickers.
